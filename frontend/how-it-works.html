How MatchPredictor works

A World Cup 2026 prediction game, built on LUKSO — no crypto experience required.

You don't need to know anything about blockchain to play. No wallet, no seed phrase, no gas fees. Just your email.

1. Sign up with just an email

Enter your email, click the link we send you — that's your login, forever. No password to remember, no wallet extension to install.

2. Already have a LUKSO Universal Profile?

You can link it during signup instead of getting a new one. If you don't know what that is, don't worry — skip this, one gets created for you automatically and invisibly.

3. Predict, for free

Pick Home / Draw / Away for any upcoming match, before kickoff. Every prediction is recorded on the LUKSO blockchain — permanently, publicly, and impossible for anyone (including us) to alter afterward. You never pay for this: the gas cost is covered automatically by MatchPredictor.

4. Win an NFT, automatically

Get it right, and an NFT prize is minted directly to your account — no claiming, no clicking, no extra step. It carries the match details (teams, result) as part of its on-chain data, and shows up in your Universal Profile like any other digital collectible.

5. Competitions

Before predicting on a league (Serie A, Premier League, etc.), you join it with one click — like joining a group. You can join up to 2 competitions at the same time. Need more? There's a link to ask for an extra slot right where you'd hit the limit.

6. Groups

Create a private group, share the invite link with friends, and choose exactly which matches count for that group's leaderboard. You can create up to 3 groups and join up to 5.

7. Testnet now, mainnet later

MatchPredictor currently runs on LUKSO Testnet — this is the trial phase, and your feedback genuinely shapes what comes next. Once the World Cup ends and new competitions begin, the game moves to LUKSO Mainnet as the definitive version.

What is LUKSO, in one sentence?

LUKSO is a blockchain network designed around digital identity — your "Universal Profile" is a bit like a social profile that you actually own, and that can hold things like these NFT prizes. You don't need to understand more than that to play.
